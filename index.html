about us

Welcome to Christ Adonai Prayer Center, a vibrant and thriving community of faith that traces its humble beginnings to a small fellowship of just five individuals. Established through the divine guidance of the Holy Spirit and the grace of God, our journey commenced in a modest house located in Ashiye, Accra. What began as a seed of fellowship has now blossomed into a thriving congregation with about 400 devoted members.

Through the unwavering commitment of our founders, the steadfast dedication of our members, and the divine favour that has guided every step, Christ Adonai Prayer Center has evolved into a spiritual haven where individuals from diverse backgrounds come together in unity and purpose. Our shared journey is a testament to the transformative power of faith Jesus Christ and the boundless possibilities that unfold when a community is united under a common vision.

Currently located in the vibrant community of Oyarifa-Teiman, Christ Adonai Prayer Center stands as a beacon of hope, love, and spiritual growth. Our mission extends beyond the walls of our sanctuary, as we actively seek to impact and enrich the lives of those in our surrounding neighbourhoods.

At Christ Adonai Prayer Center, we are more than just a congregation; we are a family bound by a shared commitment to spiritual growth, community service, and the timeless principles of love and compassion. As we look back with gratitude on our remarkable journey, we eagerly anticipate the future that God has in store for us, knowing that the best is yet to come.

Join us at Christ Adonai Prayer Center as we continue to celebrate the faithfulness of God and embark on a journey of deeper connection, purpose, and fulfilment. We invite you to be a part of our story, as together, we grow in grace and make a positive impact on the world around us.

Our vision is to proclaim the message of salvation through Jesus Christ, equipping believers with the Word of God, empowering them through prayer and the Holy Spirit, and guiding them to live a life of righteousness, faith, and love, so that all may inherit eternal life in Christ Jesus.

Frequently Asked Questions

At Christ Adonai Prayer Center, we embrace a spirit of openness and warmth. It is our joy to extend an invitation to everyone seeking spiritual guidance and solace. It is important to note that we do not charge any fees for consultations at our church. Instead, we wholeheartedly welcome individuals to come and experience the transformative presence of the Messiah.

At Christ Adonai Prayer Center, we want to clarify that we neither sell nor endorse the sale of oils within our church community. Our focus is solely on fostering a deep and unwavering trust in the power of the Holy Spirit. In alignment with our spiritual values, we believe that true strength and transformation come from a genuine connection with the Divine.

At Christ Adonai Prayer Center, we want to emphasise that there is no requirement to book an appointment before meeting with our prophet. Our doors are always open to those who wish to connect with our prophet for counsel, prayer, or spiritual insights. Whether you are a member of our congregation or a visitor, we welcome you to come as you are and seek the guidance you may be seeking.Feel free to join us during any of our services , and our prophet will be available to meet with you.
